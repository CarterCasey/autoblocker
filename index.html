MaxEnt AutoBlocker

  • Training
  • Evaluation
  • Deployment

For my final project in CS150-06, Natural Language Processing, I'm attempting to apply Maximum Entropy classification to automatically blocking accounts on Twitter. The process will involve three steps: Training, in which I gather data and select features of users on which to base the classification; Evaluation, in which the weights for those features are tuned to the problem; and Deployment, in which I make the tool available.

If you would like to help me with this project, you can go to the training page and allow me to use your following and blocked lists to train (and later evaluate) the model. Please note: I am asking you for personal information, information that twitter keeps private from outside users. Please do not give me any information if you're even a little bit uncomfortable doing so - it's just a final project, and I will find a way to make it work.
